Rules

1. Participants must not leave the game in between.🚷
2. Participants can discuss openly or personally.🗣️
3. Most voted player will be removed from game but will stay in the group.🔪⚰️

Voting Criteria:

4. Voting will be anonymous through google forms and voters list will be shared in the end of season.📝
5. Removed player cannot vote.
6. If a player fails to vote within given time then his vote is counted on himself.📵
7. If there is a tie then removed player or moderator can vote.⚔️
8. Voting will be done regularly.📢
9.If a player fails to vote for 3 consecutive days , they will be Eliminated.😶‍🌫

Immunity Criteria:⭐

10. If a player recieve Immunity then they will not be Eliminated if they are most voted.
11. A player can use Immunity only once.
12. Only 2 Immunities can be given in the game.
13. One player cannot recive Immunity twice.
14. Moderator can put status for Immunty anywhere(Insta, Whatsapp , etc...).
15. To claim Immunity message moderator on their status , 1st person to message will recieve Immunity.
16. Removed players can re-enter game through Wildcard entry based on the voting(including all members).
